Netflix in 2007

Netflix is considering changes to their business model and wants your team's input.

Current Success

  • Largest online movie rental subscription service in the US
  • Rapid subscription growth since inception
  • Fueled by DVD adoption and online rental awareness

New Development

  • January 2007: Introduced instant-watching feature for PCs
  • Plan to broaden distribution to other platforms
  • Considering expansion in January 2008

CTO

Question: What is the technological strategy for Netflix next year?


A: Modify existing website to add new services

B: Create a new website for the new services

CTO Information

  • Our recommendation service uses proprietary algorithms to compare each subscriber's title preferences with preferences of other users contained in our database.
  • We use a real-time postal address validator to help our subscribers enter correct postal addresses and to determine the additional postal address fields required to promote speedy and accurate delivery.
  • We conduct upgrades and installations of software in a manner designed to minimize disruptions to our subscribers.

Answer: B - Create a new website for the new services

CMO

Question: Netflix should use the following marketing strategy:


A: Send mails to prior DVD renters to recommend movies or TV shows on the new streaming services.

B: Use data-driven and customer-centric marketing strategies to automating the recommending system.

CMO Information

  • We promote our service to consumers through various marketing programs, including online promotions, television and radio advertising, package inserts, direct mail and other promotions with third parties.
  • All our revenues are generated in the United States, and we have no long-lived assets outside the United States.

Answer: B - Use data-driven and customer-centric marketing strategies to automating the recommending system.

COO

Question: How will the Netflix improve the operations?


A: Netflix should buy more titles.

B: Netflix should acquire more licenses.

COO Information

  • We believe our shipping centers allow us to improve the subscription experience for subscribers by shortening the transit time for our DVDs through the U.S. Postal Service.
  • For example, consumers may subscribe to HBO, rent a DVD from Blockbuster, buy a DVD from Wal-Mart or Amazon, download a movie from Apple, and subscribe to Netflix, or some combination thereof, all in the same month.

Answer: B - Netflix should acquire more licenses.

CFO

Question: What is the financial strategy for Netflix next year?


A: Netflix should allocate more funding to expand their movie collections.

B: Netflix should allocate more funding to develop their recommendation algorithms.

CFO Information

  • Revenues: $1,205,340 vs $996,660
  • Working capital: $203,956 vs $234,971
  • Total subscribers at end of period: 7,479 vs 6,316

Answer: B - Netflix should allocate more funding to develop their recommendation algorithms.
