Question: What technology should IBM develop more for year 2011?


A: More research funding needs to be put in developing software

B: More research funding needs to be put in developing cloud services

CTO Information

  • These systems form the foundation for IBM's integrated offerings, such as IBM Smart Business Storage Cloud, IBM Smart Analytics Cloud, IBM Smart Analytics System and IBM CloudBurst.
  • Retail Store Solutions. Point-of-sale retail systems (network connected cash registers) as well as solutions which connect them to other store systems.
  • Microelectronics. Semiconductor design and manufacturing primarily for use in IBM systems and storage products and for sale to external clients.

CMO

Question: How IBM's marketing budget should be spent for year 2011?


A: More budget should be put on advertising software

B: More budget should be put on advertising cloud services

CMO Information

  • The company's global capabilities include services, software, systems, fundamental research and related financing. The broad mix of businesses and capabilities are combined to provide business insight and solutions for the company's clients.
  • Approximately 60 percent of external Global Services segment revenue is annuity-based, coming primarily from outsourcing, maintenance and custom application management services arrangements.

Answer: A - More budget should be put on advertising software

COO
