Course Structure

Phase 1: Pre-Class Preparation

1. Blog posts analyzing current events through course concepts

2. Peer comments fostering discussion

Phase 2: In-Class Practice (4-Step Progression)

1. Core concept introduction (10 min) - Framework building

2. Practice cases (15 min) - Small group discussions

3. Simulation (40 min) - Real-time Simulation

4. Debrief - A separate session (45 min)

Phase 3: Post-Class Reflection

Individual reflection essays on decision-making process
