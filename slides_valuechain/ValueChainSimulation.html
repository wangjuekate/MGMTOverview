Write the value chain of The RealReal

Role: CEO

What is next year’s strategy to help The RealReal improve its balance sheet?

CEO Takeaway

Womens’ fashion/Jewery Value Chain

Primary Activities

Inbound Logistics: inventory to store luxury goods, clean and authenticate the goods

Operations: put online and match buyers

Outbound Logistics: deliver to buyers and pay consignors

Marketing & Sales: advertising the platform to attract more consignors and buyers

After-Sales Service: return and refund

Support Activities

Firm Infrastructure: Own capital or bank loan

Human Resource Management: tech engineers and local sales people

Technology Development: Online platform

Procurement: consignors sent their luxury goods

CTO

What is the technological strategy for The RealReal next year?

  • A: Hire engineers to improve the UI of the website.
  • B: Hire engineers to improve the matching algorithm of the website.

CTO Takeaways

Invest in the matching algorithm, a key technology development activity.

Role: COO

How will The RealReal improve its operations?

  • A: The RealReal should reduce its inventory.
  • B: The RealReal should partner with more luxury brands.

COO Takeaways

Reduce costs in a core primary activity by cutting inventory

CMO

The RealReal should adopt market strategy as follows:

  • A: The RealReal should include more social media in their marketing strategies.
  • B: The RealReal should host more local campaigns in their marketing strategies.

CMO Takeaways

Focus on efficient, scalable marketing (social media) that fits the online model

CFO

What is the financial strategy for The RealReal next year?

  • A: The RealReal should increase their consignor commission.
  • B: The RealReal should increase their sales price.

CFO Takeaways

Adjust the commission structure, a critical component of the firm's infrastructure and revenue model.

CHRO

What is the hiring strategy for The RealReal next year?

  • A: The RealReal should increase investing in DEI program.
  • B: The RealReal should decrease investing in DEI program.

CHRO Takeaways

Invest in culture (DEI) to support the brand's identity.

Final Decisions and Consequences

Start of turnaround:Late 2022

Focus on profitability: The company is now aiming for adjusted EBITDA profitability in 2024

Key changes:Reducing inventory, revising consignor commission structure, and focusing more on consignment than direct sales

Leadership change:CEO John Koryl joined in February 2023 to spearhead the turnaround strategy

Key Takeaways: The RealReal Turnaround

Value Chain in Action:

Value chain analyses help identify the key activities for firms to focus on.

Imitate to improve or differentiate to innovate is the critical question.

A successful turnaround requires targeted interventions across the entire value chain.
