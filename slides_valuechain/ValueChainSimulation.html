Simulation: The RealReal 2022 Turnaround

Applying Value Chain Analysis to a real-world business challenge.

Role: CEO

What is next year’s strategy to help The RealReal improve its balance sheet?

CEO Information

We offer a wide selection of authenticated, pre-owned luxury goods. A strong network effect drives our growth. We operate an online marketplace and retail stores. Major revenue lines are women’s fashion, jewelry/watches, and retail stores.

Role: CTO

What is the technological strategy for The RealReal next year?

  • A: Hire engineers to improve the UI of the website.
  • B: Hire engineers to improve the matching algorithm of the website.

CTO Information & Answer

Each item is a unique single-SKU. We use proprietary tech to optimize inbound processes (authentication, copywriting, photography). We are increasingly using tech to automate pricing, copywriting, and photo retouching. Online matching is more important than offline selling.

Answer: B - Improving the matching algorithm is a core technology challenge that directly impacts the efficiency of the marketplace.

Role: COO

How will The RealReal improve its operations?

  • A: The RealReal should reduce its inventory.
  • B: The RealReal should partner with more luxury brands.

COO Information & Answer

By making consignment easy, convenient, reliable, and fast, we unlock a vast quantity of goods. 83% of GMV is from repeat consignors. We provide an end-to-end service. Cost for authentication and inventory can be high and can be cut by reducing physical stores and inventory.

Answer: A - Reducing inventory directly addresses a major cost center in the value chain.

Role: CMO

The RealReal should adopt market strategy as follows:

  • A: The RealReal should include more social media in their marketing strategies.
  • B: The RealReal should host more local campaigns in their marketing strategies.

CMO Information & Answer

We build trust through our authentication process. We have partnerships with brands like Stella McCartney and Jimmy Choo. Our business is mostly online, not in local stores, so social media marketing is more economically efficient.

Answer: A - Social media is more efficient for a primarily online marketplace.

Role: CFO

What is the financial strategy for The RealReal next year?

  • A: The RealReal should increase their consignor commission.
  • B: The RealReal should increase their sales price.

CFO Information & Answer

GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) is increasing faster than NMV (Net Merchandise Value), indicating that costs should be cut. Consignment revenue is growing faster than direct revenue, suggesting a focus on consigning commission fees.

Answer: A - Increasing consignor commission is a direct way to improve profitability, as indicated by the GMV vs. NMV trend.

Role: CHRO

What is the hiring strategy for The RealReal next year?

  • A: The RealReal should increase investing in DEI program.
  • B: The RealReal should decrease investing in DEI program.

CHRO Information & Answer

We aspire to be designers of an equitable future through our four-pillar strategy: People, Culture, Commerce and Community. Our six Employee Resource Groups (“ERGs”) have continued to grow and evolve. Luxury brands seek identity and community; the DEI program helps maintain such a community.

Answer: A - Investing in DEI helps build the community that is essential for a luxury brand.

Key Takeaways: The RealReal Turnaround

Value Chain in Action:

  • Operations (COO): Reduce costs in a core primary activity by cutting inventory.
  • Technology (CTO): Invest in the matching algorithm, a key technology development activity.
  • Marketing (CMO): Focus on efficient, scalable marketing (social media) that fits the online model.
  • Finance (CFO): Adjust the commission structure, a critical component of the firm's infrastructure and revenue model.
  • HR (CHRO): Invest in culture (DEI) to support the brand's identity.

A successful turnaround requires targeted interventions across the entire value chain.
